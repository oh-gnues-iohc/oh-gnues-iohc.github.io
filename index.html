I am a PhD student in the Creative Vision and Multimedia Lab (CMLab) at Chung-Ang University, advised by Prof. Jihyong Oh. I received my MS from Hannam University.

My research centers on multimodal learning and agents, building systems that perceive and act across vision and language. I am broadly interested in vision-language models and how agentic reasoning can be used to tackle complex, real-world perception tasks.

Feel free to reach out at sh26choi1@gmail.com.
