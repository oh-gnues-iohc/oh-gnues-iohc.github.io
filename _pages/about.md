I am a PhD student in the Creative Vision and Multimedia Lab ([CMLab](https://cmlab.cau.ac.kr/)) at Chung-Ang University, advised by [Prof. Jihyong Oh](https://sites.google.com/view/ozbro/). I received my MS from Hannam University.

My research centers on **multimodal learning** and **agents**, building systems that perceive and act across vision and language. I am broadly interested in vision-language models and how agentic reasoning can be used to tackle complex, real-world perception tasks.

Feel free to reach out at [sh26choi1@gmail.com](mailto:sh26choi1@gmail.com).
